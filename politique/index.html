Joinz Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 20, 2025

Welcome to Joinz (the "Platform"). The Platform is provided and controlled by JOINZ SAS ("we" or "our"). As the data controller of your personal data under Article 4(7) of the General Data Protection Regulation ("GDPR"), we are committed to protecting and respecting your privacy.

To offer our services, we provide a free mobile application available for download. This policy explains how your personal data is collected, used, and shared when you access our application, products, and services (collectively, the "Services").

Please read this document carefully to understand our practices regarding your personal data and its processing.

1. Personal Data We Collect

We collect and process the personal data that you provide directly to us or that we collect automatically when you use the Platform.

  • Data collected from registered users:
    • User identifiers: unique username, email address, profile picture (optional), and phone number (optional).
    • Location data: If you enable geolocation on your device, we collect your GPS coordinates to allow you to share your real-time location with your friends or to locate meeting points.
  • Data collected automatically:
    • IP address (without precise GPS), device identifier, phone model, operating system, and application language.
    • Browsing history and interactions with the Platform (e.g., adding friends, creating events, etc.).
  • Data from third parties: If you choose to connect your social media accounts, we may receive certain data (e.g., profile picture or friends list) in accordance with the respective platform policies.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your personal data for the following purposes:

  • Providing Joinz services: enabling real-time location sharing, event creation, and connecting with your friends.
  • Improving the user experience: personalizing content and analyzing usage behaviors to optimize our services.
  • Ensuring security: preventing abuse, detecting suspicious or fraudulent activities, and protecting our users' data.
  • Keeping you informed: sending notifications about updates or changes to our services.

3. Sharing Your Data

We share your data only in the following cases:

  • With your friends: to allow them to view your location if you enable real-time sharing.
  • With our service providers: for data hosting (e.g., OVH), usage analysis, or service improvement.
  • When legally required: to comply with requests from authorities or to protect our rights.

4. Where We Store Your Data

Your personal data is hosted by our provider OVH, with servers located in Europe (France). We take measures to ensure that your data remains secure and compliant with European regulations.

5. Data Security

We use technical and organizational measures to protect your data, such as encryption and regular audits. However, transmitting data over the Internet carries risks, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data:

  • As long as your account is active. Accounts inactive for more than 12 months are deleted.
  • Some anonymized data may be retained for analytical purposes.

7. User Rights

In accordance with the GDPR, you have the following rights:

  • To access your personal data or request a copy.
  • To rectify or delete your data.
  • To withdraw your consent for location-based processing or personalized advertising.
  • To file a complaint with the relevant data protection authority (in France: CNIL).

To exercise your rights, please contact us at contact@joinz.fr.

8. Use by Minors

The Joinz application is not intended for children under 16 without parental consent. If we detect that personal data has been collected from a minor without authorization, it will be deleted.

9. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to modify this policy at any time. Changes will be communicated via the application or on our website. The update date will also be revised accordingly.

10. Contact

For any questions or requests, please write to us at: contact@joinz.fr.
